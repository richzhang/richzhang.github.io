I recently joined Adobe Research as a Research Scientist. My research interests are in computer vision, machine learning, deep learning, and sensor fusion. I was previously a PhD student at UC Berkeley, advised by Professor Alexei (Alyosha) Efros. I obtained my BS and MEng degrees from Cornell University in ECE.
